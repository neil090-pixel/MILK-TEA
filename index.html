Our Journey

Creating our business website did not happen overnight. It began as a desire to earn money and get rich. Then it became an idea that grew into action. But competition was tough as it is, so we needed a way to market our business and have an advantage against other competitors.

We realized that in today‚Äôs world, most customers look online before buying anything, so we decided that "Why not make our own website mainly focusing on our products?" So began our first steps into making our website.

The first challenge we faced was planning. We had to decide what the website should contain, like the homepage, product page, contact information, and maybe even a section for customer feedback. We discussed as a group and listed down all the things we needed. This planning stage helped us understand what kind of website we wanted to create.

Next, we moved on to the actual development. At the beginning, everything felt confusing because we were still beginners in coding or web design. We had to search for simple tutorials and ask help from others who knew more than us. Little by little, we learned how to design the layout, choose colors, and make buttons that actually worked. It was challenging, but also fun because we were learning something new.

Another important step was teamwork. Each of us had tasks: someone worked on the layout, someone gathered pictures and information, and someone checked if the website worked properly. Working together made the job easier and faster. We also helped each other when something was difficult or when someone made a mistake.

Finally, after many tries and revisions, our business website was finished. It wasn‚Äôt perfect, but it was something we were proud of. We felt accomplished knowing that we created it ourselves, starting from zero. Through this experience, we learned not only about website development, but also about patience, teamwork, and creativity. In the end, developing our business website taught us that even if something starts as a simple idea, it can turn into a meaningful project when we put effort and cooperation into it. It was a challenging process, but it helped us grow and understand the importance of adapting to modern technology.
